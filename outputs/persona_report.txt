MALL CUSTOMERS — PERSONAS & MARKETING PLAYBOOK
==================================================

🪙  Sanjay the Sensible Saver  (Sensible / Frugal)
   Low income, low spending — careful with money.
   Size: 20 customers | Avg age 46 | income $27k | spending 18
   Price-sensitive and low-engagement. Don't overspend on acquiring them; focus on light, cheap retention.
   Playbook:
     - Essentials and everyday-value messaging
     - Occasional deep-discount campaigns
     - Low-cost email newsletters only
     - Don't burn ad budget chasing them
   Best channel: Email newsletter + seasonal offers

🛍️  Aisha the Aspirational Spender  (Careless (low income, high spend))
   Loves to shop, watches the budget.
   Size: 54 customers | Avg age 25 | income $41k | spending 62
   Enthusiastic and engaged but income-limited. Great for volume if you make spending easy and affordable.
   Playbook:
     - EMI / buy-now-pay-later options
     - Value bundles & combo offers
     - Flash sales and loyalty points
     - Gamified rewards to keep them coming
   Best channel: Social media + SMS offers

💎  Priya the Power Shopper  (Premium / Target)
   High income, high spending — your VIPs.
   Size: 40 customers | Avg age 33 | income $86k | spending 82
   Affluent and happy to spend. Brand-loyal when treated well. The single most valuable group to retain.
   Playbook:
     - Exclusive early access & launches
     - Tiered loyalty / VIP program
     - Premium bundles and concierge service
     - Personalised thank-you rewards
   Best channel: App push + premium email + events

🧐  Rohan the Reluctant Earner  (Careful (high income, low spend))
   Money to spend, just not spending it here yet.
   Size: 39 customers | Avg age 40 | income $86k | spending 19
   High earning potential but low engagement. Convincing them is the biggest untapped opportunity.
   Playbook:
     - Personalised product recommendations
     - Limited-time premium trials
     - Showcase quality & exclusivity, not discounts
     - Win trust with reviews & guarantees
   Best channel: Targeted email + retargeting ads
